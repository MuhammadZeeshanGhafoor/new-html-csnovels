Description

In the 21st century, Earth witnessed the birth of a prodigious genius who mastered a myriad of arts and sciences, ranging from medicine and engineering to mathematics, microbiology, weapon design, environmental control, music, painting, dance, and martial arts. His unparalleled creativity and countless inventions earned him the title of the "Greatest Creator," as he devoted his life to helping mankind. However, when his loved one fell victim to a political conspiracy, and the governments he served turned a blind eye to the perpetrators due to their wealth and power, the "Greatest Creator" became the "Greatest Destroyer." He sought revenge, but at the cost of his own life, before realizing his full potential. But what if he were given a second chance in a different world, with all the knowledge and experience he had gained? A world where a new kind of energy could enable humans to reach the heavens? A world where he could create without the constraints of modern Earth's technology? Follow the journey of Sam as he embarks on a new life, FALSE from the limitations of his past, in a world full of possibilities.
